Pizza

Description

Pizza is an all time great in terms of affordable and tasty food

This recipe gets it to you quick in just 15 minutes with 4 simple recipes!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar + Water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Your choice of cheese and toppings

Steps

  1. Activate yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle
  5. Top your dough with your favorite cheese and toppings
  6. Bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit until golden (8 to 10 minutes)
